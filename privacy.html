Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This is a private, personal financial bookkeeping application operated by an individual (the "Owner"). It is intended solely for the Owner's own accounting and has no users other than the Owner.

Data we access

Through the Enable Banking API (PSD2 Account Information Service, read-only), the application accesses account details, balances, and transaction history only for bank accounts that the Owner has personally linked and consented to. No payments are initiated. No other person's data is accessed.

How data is used

Retrieved data is used exclusively for the Owner's personal financial record-keeping. It is stored privately on infrastructure controlled by the Owner and is never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

Consent and deletion

Access is granted via explicit bank consent and can be revoked at any time in the bank's application or by removing the linked account. The Owner may delete stored data at any time.
